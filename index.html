Our Story

A Farmer Who Thinks Like Your Family Doctor

Kappiyam Organic Farm was born from a simple belief — the food your family eats should come from someone who cares about their health, not just the harvest. We grow without chemicals, without shortcuts, and without compromise.

Located in Madampatti, Coimbatore, we currently serve 100 trusted families who receive same-day harvested produce every week — fresh from our soil to your kitchen. We keep our circle intentionally small. Limited slots available — we grow only as much as we can genuinely care for.
